# NumPy and Pandas Assignment

This repository contains assignments for learning NumPy and Pandas libraries in Python for AI Accelarator Program.

Assignment

Question 3: Price & Revenue Calculation (10 points)
Your products have these prices: [200, 350, 150, 280] NGN

Tasks:

1. Calculate daily revenue (price × quantity for each product)
2. Calculate total revenue over 30 days
3. Apply a promotional discount:
Days 1-5: 15% off all products
Days 15-20: 10% off Product A and B only
Other days: no discount
4. Calculate total revenue with discounts
5. How much discount did you give away?

RESULT

==================================================
ASSIGNMENT 3 [NUMPY]: PRICE & REVENUE CALCULATOR
==================================================

1. DAILY REVENUE:

   Products: Tomato, Rice, Pepper, Onion.
   Prices: [200 350 150 280] NGN
   Quantities sold daily: [10  3 15 12] units
   Daily revenue per product: [2000 1050 2250 3360] NGN

   Total Daily Revenue: 8,660.00 NGN

2. TOTAL REVENUE OVER 30 DAYS:
   The total revenue over 30 days is 8660 NGN * 30 days = 259,800.00 NGN

3. PROMOTIONAL DISCOUNT:
   Days 1-5: 15% off ALL products
   Days 15-20: 10% off Product A and B only
   Other days: No discount

4. TOTAL REVENUE OVER 30 DAYS (WITH DISCOUNTS):
   The total revenue with discount is 251,475.00 NGN

5. DISCOUNT ANALYSIS:
   Total discount given away: 8,325.00 NGN
   Discount as percentage of revenue: 3.20%
